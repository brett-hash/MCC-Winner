For Employers

Our Search Process

From initial consultation to successful integration, we guide you through every step with strategic insight and market intelligence.

01

Discovery & Strategy

We begin with an in-depth consultation to understand your firm's culture, strategic goals, and the specific requirements for the role. We analyze your practice dynamics, compensation structure, and what makes your opportunity compelling.

  • Confidential needs assessment
  • Competitive market analysis
  • Position specification development
  • Search strategy alignment
02

Targeted Candidate Identification

We leverage our network of 200+ placed attorneys and 180+ firm relationships to identify candidates who match both the technical requirements and cultural fit. Most candidates come through referrals and direct outreach to passive candidates.

  • Direct outreach to qualified candidates
  • Network referrals and recommendations
  • Confidential market mapping
  • Preliminary candidate screening
03

Rigorous Vetting & Assessment

Every candidate undergoes comprehensive evaluation including practice assessment, reference verification, and cultural fit analysis. We present only candidates we would hire ourselves.

  • In-depth candidate interviews
  • Practice capability assessment
  • Reference and credential verification
  • Detailed candidate presentations
04

Interview Coordination & Negotiation

We manage the entire interview process, provide strategic counsel to both parties, and facilitate negotiations to reach mutually beneficial terms.

  • Interview scheduling and logistics
  • Candidate preparation and coaching
  • Offer negotiation and structuring
  • Resignation and transition guidance
05

Integration & Follow-Up

Our commitment doesn't end at acceptance. We provide 90-day integration support with structured check-ins to ensure long-term placement success.

  • Start date coordination
  • 30/60/90-day check-ins
  • Integration support and troubleshooting
  • 90-day placement guarantee

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything You Need to Know

Common questions about working with MCC Advisors, our process, and what makes us different.

For Employers

How much do your services cost?

+

Our fee structure depends on the type of engagement. For retained searches (typically partner-level or C-suite roles), we work on an exclusive basis with fees paid in installments. For contingency placements (associate and mid-level positions), we're paid only upon successful hire. We'll discuss the specific fee arrangement during our initial consultation based on your needs.

What's your average time to fill a position?

+

Timeline varies by seniority and practice area, but our average is 6-12 weeks for most placements. Partner-level searches typically take 3-4 months. We prioritize quality over speed, but our network allows us to move efficiently when the right candidate exists.

Do you guarantee your placements?

+

Yes. We offer a 90-day guarantee on all placements. If a candidate leaves or is terminated within 90 days for any reason other than firm-wide layoffs, we'll replace them at no additional fee. Our 97% two-year retention rate means we rarely need to invoke this guarantee.

How do you source candidates?

+

We maintain an active network of 200+ placed attorneys and relationships with 180+ firms. Most of our candidates come through referrals, our direct outreach to passive candidates, and attorneys who specifically seek us out. We don't rely on job boards—85% of our opportunities never appear publicly.

What markets do you serve?

+

While we're based in Sioux Falls, we place attorneys nationwide with concentrations in major legal markets including New York, Chicago, Washington DC, Los Angeles, San Francisco, and Boston. We also work with firms in emerging markets and have experience with remote placements.

For Candidates

Do I have to pay anything to work with you?

+

No. Our services are 100% free for candidates. We're paid by the hiring organization when we successfully place you. There are never any fees, costs, or obligations for attorneys using our services.

Will you share my information without my permission?

+

Never. Confidentiality is paramount. We will not share your resume, discuss your interest in making a move, or contact anyone on your behalf without your explicit authorization for each specific opportunity. Your career search remains completely confidential.

I'm not actively looking. Should I still reach out?

+

Absolutely. The best opportunities often come when you're not actively searching. We work with many passive candidates and can alert you when something exceptional arises. Even if you're just curious about your market value or potential opportunities, we're happy to have a confidential conversation.

How many opportunities will you present to me?

+

Quality over quantity. We don't blast your resume to every open position. We take time to understand your goals, practice strengths, and cultural preferences, then present only opportunities that genuinely align. You might see 2-3 highly targeted options rather than 20 generic ones.

What if I'm interested in going in-house?

+

We place both law firm and in-house roles. We have strong relationships with Fortune 500 legal departments and understand the transition from private practice to in-house. We can help you evaluate whether an in-house move aligns with your long-term goals and comp expectations.

About MCC Advisors

What makes MCC different from other recruiters?

+

Three things: (1) We focus exclusively on legal recruiting, so we understand the nuances of law firm culture, partnership tracks, and practice dynamics. (2) Our 97% two-year retention rate proves we prioritize long-term fit over quick placements. (3) We operate with complete transparency—no games, no surprises, just honest counsel.

Are your recruiters former attorneys?

+

Our team brings deep expertise in the legal market through years of specialized recruiting experience. We've built strong relationships with Am Law 100 firms, Fortune 500 legal departments, and elite boutiques, giving us insider knowledge of firm cultures, compensation structures, and hiring priorities.

Do you handle practice group moves?

+

Yes. Multi-attorney team transitions require strategic coordination and careful planning. We have experience facilitating practice group moves and understand the unique dynamics of partner groups relocating together. These engagements are typically handled on a retained basis.

How do I get started?

+

Simple. Reach out through our contact page, call us at (605) 759-1835, or email info@themccway.com. We'll schedule a confidential conversation to discuss your needs or goals. No obligation, no pressure—just an honest discussion about how we might help.
